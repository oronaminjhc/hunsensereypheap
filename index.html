About Us

Hun Sen Sereypheap High School is a respected public secondary school in Takhmao, Kandal. We offer a comprehensive curriculum from lower to upper secondary with Khmer literature, mathematics, science, foreign languages, social studies, and computer science. With supportive teachers, a safe and disciplined environment, and diverse clubs and sports, students build knowledge, character, and confidence.
