Welcome from Principal

Hun Sen Sereypheap High School is a respected public secondary school in Takhmao, Kandal. We offer a comprehensive curriculum from lower to upper secondary with Khmer literature, mathematics, science, foreign languages, social studies, and computer science. With supportive teachers, a safe and disciplined environment, and diverse clubs and sports, students build knowledge, character, and confidence. We prepare learners for the national exam (Bac II), higher education, and community leadership‚Äîhelping every student develop the skills and values to contribute positively to Cambodia's future.

National Exam (Bac II)

Exam Overview

The Bac II examination is Cambodia's national high school graduation exam. Students must pass this exam to receive their high school diploma and be eligible for university admission.

Frequently Asked Questions

The Bac II exam is typically held in August each year. Exact dates are announced by the Ministry of Education.

The exam covers Khmer Literature, Mathematics, Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology), Social Studies, and Foreign Languages.

Each subject is scored out of 20 points. Students need to achieve an average of 10/20 to pass the exam.
