National Exam (Bac II)

Exam Overview

The Bac II examination is Cambodia's national high school graduation exam. Students must pass this exam to receive their high school diploma and be eligible for university admission.

Frequently Asked Questions

The Bac II exam is typically held in August each year. Exact dates are announced by the Ministry of Education.

The exam covers Khmer Literature, Mathematics, Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology), Social Studies, and Foreign Languages.

Each subject is scored out of 20 points. Students need to achieve an average of 10/20 to pass the exam.
